About

I am a current UMBC graphic design major, set to graduate in Spring 2016. I specialize in logo design and 2D development but I also have experience in web design, through use of HTML and Java. I hope to use graphic design to help communicate ideas effectively in the corporate world while invigorating and refreshing familiar graphic motifs, to innovate eye-catching designs.
